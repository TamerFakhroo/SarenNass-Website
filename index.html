Who We Are

About Us

Established in 2003, Sarens Nass Middle East (SNME) is a joint venture between Bahrain-based Nass Group and Belgium-based Sarens NV. For decades, we have been at the forefront of engineering innovation, serving as the trusted reference for heavy lift and transport solutions across the Middle East and beyond.

Our Mission

To focus on safety, quality, and reliability with respect to our operations, employees, equipment, and our environment, assuring our customers services of international standards.

Our Vision

To deliver comprehensive and effective Heavy Lift, Shift & Access Services by leveraging our global expertise and local knowledge to become the industry leading reference.

Our Values

01

Global reputation, local expertise

With our unparalleled local reach and global experience, Sarens Nass sets the standard for world-class heavy lift solutions, crane rental, rig moves, shutdown services, stevedoring, and material handling.

02

The region's largest heavy lift and transport fleet

Our 4,000+ strong fleet of heavy lift cranes and transport equipment is the largest and most comprehensive in the region, providing clients with seamless rental and swift deployment of rigorously maintained, safe, and modern equipment.

03

Superior safety standards

We are committed to the highest SHEQ standards to ensure we protect the people, assets, and environments in which we operate. We are proud of our rigorous safety training and regular equipment inspection and maintenance.

04

Enduring values, lasting impact

We believe in providing swift, safe, and sustainable operations that exceed client expectations, safeguard our environment, and empower local communities. Our commitment to corporate social responsibility drives us to create greater wellbeing and prosperity in the communities we serve.
